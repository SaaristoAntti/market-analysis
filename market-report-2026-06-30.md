Aschenbrenner is running a "barbell" portfolio: long the physical infrastructure powering AI (electricity, data centers, crypto miners), hedged with massive puts against semiconductor stocks whose valuations may already price in the AI capex narrative. ### Important Caveat
13F filings report put/call **notional value** (total value of underlying shares), not the actual premium paid. The directional intent (long puts vs. short puts) cannot be confirmed from 13F data alone.
